Safety Rules

Ensure you follow these safety rules when operating or maintaining your vehicle:

  • Always wear your seatbelt.
  • Follow traffic signals and road signs.
  • Maintain a safe distance from other vehicles.
  • Regularly check tire pressure and fluid levels.
  • Never use a mobile phone while driving.
